About me

I am a PhD student in the Institute for Computational Science, University of Zurich and I am also affiliated with the EcoVision Lab, ETH Zurich. My supervisors are Prof. Jan Dirk Wegner and Prof. Konrad Schindler. My research interests lie in computer vision, remote sensing, deep learning, and global vegetation parameter estimation.
